Birds

There are thousands of bird species. Each of them has their own eating habits.
Each of them has their own eating habits. Other fruits, nectar and even in some cases meat. However, due to the lack of information and the ease and lower cost,
many breeders only feed their birds with seeds – a mistake that can be very serious.

Ready-to-eat feeds can have different amounts of macronutrients such as proteins and fats.
Birds, however, need food balanced, according to the needs of each species.
Careful carelessness usually causes digestive and bone problems - due to an inadequate diet containing nutrients such as calcium and phosphorus

Birds can also suffer from respiratory, infectious and parasitic problems.
The people in charge must keep them sheltered from the cold, the wind and offer the environmental conditions so that they can recover.
Behavioral disorders, such as depression, are also quite common in captive-bred birds.
Some animals even practice self-mutilation, plucking their own feathers due to stress.
Some illnesses can increase the problem, making the feathers even more fragile.
