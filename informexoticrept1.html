Reptiles

Turtles, Tortoises, iguanas and snakes can suffer from poor handling.
Skin, hoof, pneumonia and nutritional deficiencies
are common in these species when raised outside their natural habitat.
Some illnesses are caused by poor hygiene, incorrect temperature and humidity.
Therefore, it is essential to consult the specialized veterinarian, to know what environmental conditions are necessary for each species.
