Preparation

The arrival of a dog completely changes the dynamics of the house! It takes calm, patience and a lot of organization.
O cão pode trazer mudanças nos hábitos familiares e rotinas e, por isso, é fundamental que esteja preparado para a sua chegada.
Assim como adotar um cão ou gato, é recomendado contactar um veterinário especializado.
Dessa forma, o profissional poderá esclarecer condições necessárias para manter a saúde e bem estar do animal.


Introduction of the house:

If you're a first-rate owner, know that reserving a place in the house to be your pet's starting spot is a great start!
This helps to create referral and discipline, it is important that they have full access to a corner where they can go about their business.
In this place you can leave a guard for example or even a sandbox because some small breeds accept it.


Ration:

Care regarding the animal's feeding is extremely important, there are several types of
rations (moist, dry, premium, sterilized, delicate, etc.), right amounts of food, foods allowed or prohibited for your animal.
So when buying the feed, keep an eye on the packaging!
They indicate whether it is for youngsters, seniors, adults, neutered, large or small size and other important characteristics.


Adapt the routine to your pet:

Understanding that the dynamics of the house must change is the first step, the second is adapt your routine to that of your pet.
A walk, for example, is the main activity. physical fitness of dogs and it is essential that you have time to walk yours every day.
Trying to feed him at the same times as his meals and getting him used to his bedtime are both essential.


All these tips are for setting your new pet dog.
